Assignment Title: Development of a Digital Entrepreneurship Plan
Overview
In this assignment, students will develop a comprehensive digital entrepreneurship plan that
demonstrates their ability to identify opportunities, design innovative digital solutions, and
communicate their vision effectively to potential investors. The project integrates key aspects
of market analysis, strategic direction, innovation design, and digital tools integration.
Objectives
By completing this assignment, students will be able to:
1. Apply strategic planning frameworks (vision, mission, SMART goals, KPIs, scenario-
based planning).
2. Design an innovative digital business model using the Business Model Canvas
(BMC).
3. Identify opportunities for digital value creation and platform scalability.
4. Integrate suitable digital tools and technologies to enhance performance and
competitiveness.
5. Communicate their plan effectively through a professional investor-style pitch.
Assignment Requirements
Part 1: Written Report (70%)
Students must prepare a professional report (2,500- 3,000 words) structured as follows:
1. Executive Summary – concise overview of the digital venture.
2. Strategic Direction – vision, mission, goals, and KPIs.
3. Market & Environmental Analysis – using SWOT/PESTEL and relevant data.
4. Innovation Design – describe the innovative product/service/process and its value
proposition.
5. Digital Business Model – complete a Business Model Canvas with justification for
each block.
6. Digital Tools Integration – discuss the technologies enabling scalability (e.g.,
platforms, analytics, automation).
7. Scenario-Based Planning – outline alternative futures and strategic adaptability.
8. Implementation Roadmap & Financial Overview – key actions, timelines, basic
financials.
9. Conclusion & Reflection – lessons learned and venture potential.
Formatting: APA style, professional tone, visuals encouraged (charts, BMC diagram, KPIs
dashboard, etc.).
Part 2: Pitch Presentation (20%)
Students will present their business concept in a 15-minute investor-style pitch, simulating
real-world funding conditions.
• Highlight the problem, solution, market opportunity, and innovation.
• Present the Business Model Canvas and strategic goals.
• Emphasize the digital tools that create competitive advantage.
• Use clear visuals (slides, infographics, prototype screenshots).
Part 3: Supporting Visuals (10%)
Students should include professional-quality visual materials to enhance both the written and
oral components. These may include:
• Business model canvas
• Customer journey maps
• Infographics or wireframes
• Product concept visuals or mock-ups
